Iruttu Araiyil Murattu Kuthu transl.Fierce masturbation or sexual encounter punch, euphemism in a dark room is a 2018 Indian Tamil-language adult horror "comedy" film written and directed by Santhosh P. Jayakumar and produced by K. E. Gnanavel Raja. The film stars Gautham Karthik and VJ Sha Ra, with Yashika Aannand, Chandrika Ravi, and Vaibhavi Shandilya are in the female leads. The music was composed by Balamurali Balu with cinematography by Ballu and editing by Prasanna GK. The film was released on 4 May 2018.
