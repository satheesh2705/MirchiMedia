Pizza 3: The Mummy is a 2023 Indian Tamil-language horror thriller film directed by Mohan Govind, starring Raveena Daha, Ashwin Kakumanu and Pavithrah Marimuthu. Marking the third film in the Pizza film series,[1] it was released on 28 July 2023 and received average reviews. The film grossed around 5 Crore and was considered a decent hit.At the start of the movie, a man comes home with a gift for his daughter, which is a Barbie Doll. His son also finds a camera. The husband then pulls out a mini-mummy sarcophagus statue, a miniature resemblance of the one that of King Tut. His wife jokingly tells him that he is taking too much of these. After some time of taking the a Mummy statue out, the electricity goes out in the house. The wife lights up candles in the house, while the son is recording with the camera. The daughter then asks for a bedtime story. When the dad tries to tell an Indian story of an elephant , the second son requests a horror story.
